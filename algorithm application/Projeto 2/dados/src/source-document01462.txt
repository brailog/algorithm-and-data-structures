The Safest and the Most Dangerous Cars and Trucks for 2005
by SixWise.com
According to the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety, 42,815 people died in motor vehicle crashes in 2002. Driving a safe vehicle is one of the best ways to protect yourself and your family while you're on the road. If you or someone you know is in the market for a car or truck in 2005, the vehicle's safety should, of course, be a top priority.
Below are the top three safest and least safe vehicles in the seven most common consumer vehicle categories. This is a cumulative ranking based on the results of multiple testing organizations including the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety, Consumer Reports and Safe Car Guide.
