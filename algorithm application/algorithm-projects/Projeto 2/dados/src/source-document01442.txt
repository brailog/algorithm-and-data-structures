PAN AM HISTORICAL FOUNDATION Notice of 2007 Annual Meeting of Members
Notice is hereby given that the Sixteenth Annual Meeting of Members of the Pan Am Historical Foundation will be held on Friday, April 4, 2008 at 2 p.m. at R.M. Smythe, Two Rector Street, 12th floor, New York, NY, for the following purposes:
(1) To elect directors to serve for the ensuing year and until their successors are elected.
(2) To transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment thereof.
Pamela Hamlon Recording Secretary
New York, New York March 3, 2008

Sir Richard Branson ReceivesClipper Heritage Award
The Pan Am Historical Foundation recently awarded its to Sir Richard Branson, Founder and Chairman of the Virgin Group Ltd., for his environmental initiatives.Clipper Heritage Award
The award honors Sir Richard's "significant contribution to Humanity involving aviation and exemplifying the vision, innovation and adventure that was Pan American World Airways." And recognizes "his unique initiatives as an airline owner to meet the challenge of global warming and minimize the environmental impact of his businesses."
"Sir Richard has committed profits from the transport companies (airlines and trains) of his Virgin Group, estimated at $3 billion over the next 10 years, to renewable energy initiatives to combat global warming."
"In addition, he has launched the , offering a prize of $25 million for a commercially viable design which results in the net removal of greenhouse gases from the atmosphere each year for at least 10 years, so as to help stabilize the Earth's climate"Virgin Earth Challenge
