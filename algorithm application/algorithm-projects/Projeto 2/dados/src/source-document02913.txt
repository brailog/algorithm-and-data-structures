Arkadelphia Youth Sports Complex
You should enter the from Country Club Road in Arkadelphia. From Arkadelphia go north on North 26th street until the end of the street. Turn right onto Country Club Road and then make an immediate left into the Youth Sports Complex. From Caddo Valley enter Arkadelphia on Hwy 67 and turn right onto Country Club Road at Pizza Hut. Proceed down Country Club road until after you pass a car wash, laundromat and an apartment complex and turn right into the Youth Sports Complex. The soccer fields are on the far side of the Youth Sports Complex from the entrance road. If you follow the main road through the complex, you will find the fields. Arkadelphia Youth Sports Complex
